What

Star Realm VR is a virtual reality multiplayer sandbox-building and social platform that allows you to build and decorate floating islands with friends.

Why

I want to build a home where I can meet my friends no matter how far apart. I want to utilize the power of VR/AR to reduce the cost of attending the event and meeting with friends.

Trailer

Problem

We are outgunned, outmanned!

Framework

Persona

As a startup, our resources and budget are limited, but we possess the unique flexibility of a small company. After facing several obstacles, we decided to focus all our efforts on social interaction. I designed a purpose-driven core architecture, where every feature aids player interaction, categorized into direct and indirect assistance. Finally, based on our goals, we developed a series of reward and punishment mechanisms for players.

Research

Our audience decides what we are

We first identified our target customers through various research reports and created user personas. Then, based on the target customers and the comprehensive capabilities of our team, we formulated corresponding plans for the art style, operational logic, and platform functions that this group is interested in, and found relevant references.

Result

为了给玩家提供丰富的搭建选择，我们舍弃了传统的资产流程，选了了极为高效的程序化资产流程。我们首先列出了各个品类所对应的尺寸，制定我们的模型标准。然后我们通过程序化建模的方式将长短不一，样式百变的资产规范化，同时我们还能快速的变化资产的风格，以提供不同主题的资产。

Reflection

Know what you can do and then decide what to do

The prolonged duration of this project is primarily due to our unclear understanding of our capabilities and limits. We set overly ambitious goals and underestimated the complexity of the tasks. Team members came and went, each arriving with enthusiasm but eventually leaving as their passion waned over time. As entrepreneurs, this cycle is disheartening, yet we understand that perseverance is the key to eventual success.
