What

Welcome to Neon City, a virtual reality city-building game that combines the charm of block toys with cutting-edge hand-tracking technology. Create and explore your digital city in 360 degrees with a unique retro-inspired art style, with the added bonus of experiencing your unique metropolis in a 1:1 scale.

Why

I want to explore the power of procedural modeling and VR to build a virtual city where Neon City offers emergent possibilities based on the player's motivation as they create Neon City. Additionally, I am eager to explore how VR toys can enhance the experiences of children in the future.

Reference

Result

In order to produce enough building materials in three months, we used procedural modeling. Under the guidance of the art director, the artists first prepared various fragmented building materials, and then used scripts in Houdini and Unity to piece these materials together. This ensured uniformity while also adding randomness within the consistency.

Reflection

Just do it and then iterate on it

I've come to realize a vital lesson in both life and work: it's essential to take proactive steps before situations become overly complex. This approach aligns with the old wisdom, "Learning without thought leads to confusion; thought without learning leads to idleness." It highlights the delicate balance between contemplation and action.

In the pursuit of perfection or the 'optimal' solution, we often find ourselves trapped in a cycle of overthinking, leading to paralysis by analysis. This constant quest for the best method can result in missed opportunities and stalled progress. It's a common trap, especially in environments that demand quick decision-making and adaptability.
